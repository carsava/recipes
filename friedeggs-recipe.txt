Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - salt
 - 50 ml olive oil


Directions:
  1. Dump the oil in a pan, put it on the fire until the oil is hot.
  2. Lays the eggs one by one carefully. Eggs should be temperature environment, never from the refrigerator.
  3. With a slotted spoon move some oil pouring over.
  4. Carefully remove the eggs dripping oil well.
  5. Take a little salt over.
